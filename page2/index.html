What the fuck is escapism?


I wasn’t going to do the “dictionary says” cliché until I looked at Wiktionary, and nearly fell out of my metaphorical seat. (Actual ass was fine.)



“Escapism: An inclination to escape from routine or reality into fantasy. A genre of book, film etc. that one uses to indulge this tendency. The performance of an escape artist.”



Indulge non-reality.

Even the dictionary is shaming our imagining. Our “what if?”
