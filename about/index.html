Escapism is a lie: a manifesto and commentary about the stigmatization of escapism in literature and books by e.d.e. bell.

E.D.E. Bell (she/her or e/em) is a writer and editor in Ferndale, Michigan. In addition to several wonderful stories in the works with her small press, Atthis Arts, Emily is releasing two of her own books in 2024: the third novel in eir Alyssia quiet, high fantasy world, Storm Tree, and the conclusion to eir queer comedic psychological weird duology, Just Chad. You can follow her adventures at  edebell.com.

Bell, E.D.E. 2024. “Escapism is a Lie.” Apex Book Company.
