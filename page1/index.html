Sure, both exist.

and why?

I’ve long given a partial answer: my evolving understanding of the ways that it is and can be both. I was an engineer with engineering training, and this was an answer I could parse. However, as my understanding of the genre and the world grows (very non-coincidentally, together) I have realized with a bit of internal fury—



the question is a lie in the first place. An intentional lie. Posed to us by oppressors as another roadblock to our understanding. To our connections.
