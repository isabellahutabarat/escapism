Genres are useful! They are ways to describe our writing, and who might like it.

But they cannot be bins.

They cannot be used to gatekeep.



When we can, when we’re able—as readers of xpfic and all things weird and wonderful, let’s remember the revolution is in the authenticity of our hearts.


In our messy selves, our weird stories, the way we speak, and also in the way we perceive.
